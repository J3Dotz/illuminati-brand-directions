1B.6 · Favicon Mark

Choosing a brand mark

The favicon is the smallest expression of the brand — 16 pixels of recognition. Two marks are presented for decision: the Swan, a ten-year heritage asset, and the Geometric 'a', derived from the wordmark system built in 2023.

Mark A · Swan

Abstracted from the swan lamp that has defined the brand for over a decade. Three curvilinear forms in balanced tension — recognisable as organic, distinctive, and immediately ownable independent of the wordmark. The heritage choice.

Mark B · Geometric 'a'

Derived from the rectangle-constructed wordmark system. Every form is a right angle — consistent with the geometric logic that runs through the entire identity. The system-native choice, created when the website was redesigned in 2023.
